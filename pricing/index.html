Giclée prints are made using acid free archival papers and high dynamic range pigment inks. Each print should last at least 100 yrs and should stand up to generational passing down. Put simply, pigment-based particles form a thicker layer on a substrate than dyes.

Prints are offered with or without a .5” white border. If the end goal is matting and framing then we would include the .5” border and price according to the media size (image size plus border). 1-3 day turnaround.

When sending image files please send the highest resolution file that you have. The better the image we
start with, the better the final product will look. We will work with almost any file type but prefer the following:

  • 300 DPI
  • .TIF
  • SRGB Color Profile

Disclaimers

Due to copyright laws, we will need proof of artist / photographer consent to print works that are not
originating from you. Exceptions include pieces that are 100+ yrs old or in the public domain.
Print pricing may vary due to supply chain issues and increased prices of our papers, matboards, foam core and frames. Unfortunately these costs must be passed on in order for our business to survive.
