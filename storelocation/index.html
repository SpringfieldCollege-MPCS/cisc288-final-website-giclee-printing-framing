Directions

Travel onto Shawinigan Dr. (Chicopee Falls)

Turn Right onto West Street follow the street down

Driving through West Street you will see a huge building between a river and a canal. (Chicopee River)

Drive all the way down towards a plaza

Their will be a sign that says Indian Orchard Mills on the left side

Their will be a huge parking lot and once you reach the building. Their will be a series of door numbers.

Drive towards door number 18.

Once you reach door 18 the doors will be locked. So you would have call Lisa in order to get inside. Once unlocked their will be 3 flights of stairs. Their will be signs that guide you towards the frame store.

We are operating by appointment only so please contact us to schedule. We also offer customized services to meet your needs if you are unable to visit us in our shop.
