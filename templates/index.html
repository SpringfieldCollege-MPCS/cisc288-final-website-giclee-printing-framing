What We Do

Here at Giclée Printing & Framing, we develop strong relationships with our customers, some who travel for hours to bring their artwork in to be scanned, proofed and when approved, reproduced as Giclée prints. Personal service is hard to find in any area of business however that is one of the most important factors in making sure all our customers are completely satisfied with our services and products.

Giclée Printing & Framing of New England serves companies and individuals from all over the U.S.A and internationally, by providing high quality imaging, Giclée printing, custom picture framing, photography services, and professional consulting services. We are conveniently located between Springfield and Worcester.

About us

Nancy Bryant, herself an artist and photographer, explored different art techniques until settling on digital imaging as a medium. This led to the exploration of new technologies and the discovery of the Giclée process. This process of imaging is done with archival pigment based inks on acid free media using state-of-the-art technology.


Inspired to move forward with this blend of technology and art, in 2003 she opened Giclée of New England, Inc. The services offered range from image capture to large format digital output. The frame shop offers custom framing. The gallery offers original art and limited edition prints featuring talented local artists and artisans. Over the years, Nancy developed quite a reputation for her expertise in art, photography, imaging services, digital editing and photo restoration.
