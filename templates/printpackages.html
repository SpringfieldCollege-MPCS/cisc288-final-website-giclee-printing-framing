These packages are made for the artist or photographer who wants to sell reproductions of their work in a classy, standard size package that is ready to be popped into a standard sized frame.

Each package includes the Giclée Print on Premium Fine Art or Photo Paper. Choice of White Linen, Off White, Black Linen or Black Onyx mat colors. Each package is enclosed in a clear acid free sleeve and includes the print; matted and mounted on foam core, and a customized “Certificate of Authenticity”. All materials are acid free and archival…made to last for generations to come.

After making sure you are getting the highest quality archival print available, we hand cut the print, custom cut your mat (with the same hands), align, attach and put carefully into the clear sleeve. We even use linen tape to attach your print to the foam core…something you wouldn’t see.

Disclaimers

Due to copyright laws, we will need proof of artist / photographer consent to print works that are not originating from you. Exceptions include pieces that are 100+ yrs old or in the public domain.
Print pricing may vary due to supply chain issues and increased prices of our papers, matboards, foam core and frames. Unfortunately these costs must be passed on in order for our business to survive.
